Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this platform?

This platform connects language learners with native speakers for mutual language practice and cultural exchange.

How does the language exchange work?

Learners and native speakers connect through chat or video calls to have conversations and practice their respective languages.

Is this platform suitable for beginners?

This platform caters to learners of all proficiency levels, including beginners.

How can I find a language partner?

Create an account, fill out your preferences and learning goals, and the platform will match you with suitable language partners.
